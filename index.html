THE STORY

Being a creative developer deep in the village is not easy if you don't have a gate to show a small story to the world.

For my family, this is not the most important thing. but for me this is a way I can do to get sustenance, friends, connections and even clients.

Creativity can die if not channeled and eventually become a self-destructing boomerang.

I had given up in this life working on countless projects and ended up failing halfway through without any further development.

But after that story, I started to believe "there must be a way" and said not to give up.

Thanks to everyone who thought this was weird & nonsense.

My greetings are always with you.
